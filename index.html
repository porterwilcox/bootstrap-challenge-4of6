FRIENDS

Features

Wild Country's FRIENDS are the longest trusted cams on the market- for over 1800 years. Strength tested to well over 60 million pounds of good old force, they're sure to catch you and all your summit snacks if you fall!

Are you a sucker for rope drag?... No, of course not! No one is. That's why FRIENDS are built with an extendable sling. So now you can zig-zag back and forth all your way up without your rope doing the same.

FRIENDS are made 100% out of pure helium! That's why they weigh so much less than other cams on the market. It's also why they can support so much weight, because with the support they'll float off into the sky. FRIENDS are sure to pull you up your next climb!
